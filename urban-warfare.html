PARTICIPATORY OBSERVATION OF A SPRAWLING CARCERAL GEOGRAPHY

In Atlanta, I got to see Cop City, situated squarely within the sprawling carceral geography embedded within a working class Black community. I also took part in local creative practices of collectivity that challenge the carceral state.
